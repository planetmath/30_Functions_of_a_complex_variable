unit disk


The unit disk in the complex plane, denoted Δ, is defined as {z∈ℂ:|z|<1}. The unit circle, denoted ∂⁡Δ or S1 is the boundary {z∈ℂ:|z|=1} of the unit disk Δ. Every element z∈∂⁡Δ can be written as z=ei⁢θ for some real value of θ.
